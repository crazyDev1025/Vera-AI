The two most trusted roles in the Vera DAO are the validators and the consuls

Validators

Validators are a sort of 2FA for the voting process. After the voting ended, they review the transparency and honesty of the decision. Validators have the final say in accepting the proposal

Council members

Council members have the highest judicial authority, representing the DAO in the Web2 world. They get appointed and removed by the decision of a DAO. The appointed Council members automatically become Validators.
