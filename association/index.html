Vera protocol is not only smart contracts. We see Vera DAO as a sort of

Hammurabi Code of DAOs

If The DAO or MakerDAO could be called the "Original Eve" of DAOs in how others followed their blueprint (or forked their code), we see Vera DAO as a sort of Hammurabi Code of DAOs. Just like Hammurabi's code is a basic set of laws and principles from nearly 4000 years ago that still forms the basis of the governance of human civilization, we want Vera's DAO AI to form the basis of DAO governance for millennia to come.
